Summer is over, the timetables are out, and your first lectures are on the horizon. As September draws to a close maybe you are settling in to your new student flat or learning to love your current one for the second time. Either way the words "freshers fortnight" will no doubt conjure distant memories of drunken fancy-dress, Burlington Bertie, and french supermarkets.

But freshers fortnight need not be a distant memory. We are offering you, the upper years, a chance to rekindle your fresher spirit and embark on that right of passage all over again. For just £10 you can relive Bloomsbury Bowl, Proud, Salvador & Amanda, the Hoxton Pony, and our very special Regents Park Zombie Game. Or how about that Freshers' Fortnight Classic, the Olswang Boat Cruise? A ticket can be yours for just £20 (£25 for non-members).

We look forward to seeing you relive freshers and start your new academic year with a bang!

All the best,

President, UCL Law Society
