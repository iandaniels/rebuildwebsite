Simmons & Simmons has a reputation for offering a superior legal service, wherever and whenever it is required. Our lawyers’ high quality advice and the positive working atmosphere with fully integrated teams working through offices in Europe, the Middle East and Asia have won admiration and praise from both the legal community and our business clients. 

RANGE OF CLIENT SERVICES

We offer our clients a full range of legal services across numerous industry sectors. We have a particular focus on the world’s fastest growing sectors, that is: Financial Institutions; Asset Management & Investment Funds; Energy & Infrastructure; Life Sciences; and Technology, Media & Telecommunications (TMT).We provide a wide choice of service areas in which our lawyers can specialise. These include Corporate & Commercial; Information, Communications & Technology; Dispute Resolution; Employment; EU, Regulatory & Competition; Financial Markets; IP; Projects; Real Estate; Corporate Tax and Corporate Pensions.

WHAT SIMMONS & SIMMONS ARE LOOKING FOR

We are looking for ambitious, focused individuals with law or non-law degrees.  Academic achievement is key, and you will need to achieve at least a 2.1 in your degree, but we also want rounded individuals who can fit smoothly into our teams as trainees need to work with people in our international offices and with clients throughout the world.
Show us evidence of a rich ‘life experience’ as well as examples of your intellectual capabilities and we will provide you with everything you need to become a successful member of our firm.

The firm offers around 40 training contracts each year and are currently recruiting for the September 2015 and March 2016 intakes. Our six month seat system enables you to gain hands on experience in four different areas of our business, working with other lawyers, gaining knowledge in different service areas and dealing with our all important clients. 

We also offer around 90 Vacation Scheme placements per year through our Spring, Summer and Winter schemes. Our vacation schemes provide a unique insight into working life at Simmons & Simmons.
